About Todo List

Welcome to the Todo List application! Here's how to use it:

  1. Click on "Add a Todo" to open the form.
  2. Enter a title for your todo in the "Todo Title" field.
  3. Describe your todo in the "Todo Description" field.
  4. Click on "Submit" to add the todo to your list.

That's it! You can now start organizing your tasks with the Todo List.
